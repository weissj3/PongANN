Pong Vs a Neural Net

Use the up and down arrows to play pong against the current best neural networks in the training set. This list of neural networks is updated every 5 minutes. These updates are automatically recognized by your browser and do not require a page refresh. The code for the neural network training and this webpage can be found at https://github.com/weissj3/PongANN.
